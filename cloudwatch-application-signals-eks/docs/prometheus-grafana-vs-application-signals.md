# Prometheus/Grafana vs Application Signals

- Prometheus/Grafana: strong when you want to design collection, labels, dashboards, and retention yourself.
- Application Signals: strong when you want quick service-level visibility into latency, faults, errors, dependencies, and SLOs.

They are not mutually exclusive. Many teams can use both: Prometheus/Grafana for flexible observability design, and Application Signals for faster service triage.
